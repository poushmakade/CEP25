Our Mission

At HelpingHands, we are committed to improving the learning environment for students in need. During our visit to NANDANWAN DURBAL MANASK MULANCHI SHALA, we identified several areas that require immediate attention:

  • The dull and uninspiring colors of the school walls, which fail to create a positive and engaging environment for students.
  • A lack of basic infrastructure, such as proper seating arrangements, clean classrooms, and recreational facilities.
  • The absence of a vibrant and motivating atmosphere that encourages students to learn and grow.

Being a government school, it struggles with limited resources to address these issues. We believe that every child deserves a nurturing and inspiring environment to thrive academically and personally.

To address these challenges, our mission is to:

  • Repaint the school walls with bright and cheerful colors to create a welcoming and positive atmosphere.
  • Provide essential infrastructure, such as desks, chairs, and learning materials, to enhance the quality of education.
  • Introduce recreational and creative spaces to foster holistic development among students.

Your contributions will directly impact the lives of these students by helping us achieve these goals. Together, we can create a brighter future for the children of NANDANWAN DURBAL MANASK MULANCHI SHALA.
