About NANDANWAN DURBAL MANASK MULANCHI SHALA

NANDANWAN DURBAL MANASK MULANCHI SHALA was established in 1960 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in an urban area, specifically in the URC 1 block of Nagpur district, Maharashtra. The school consists of Grades from 1 to 4 and is co-educational. It does not have an attached pre-primary section. The school operates privately and does not use the school building as a shift-school. Marathi is the medium of instruction in this school, and it is approachable by an all-weather road. The academic session in this school starts in April.
